Netlify SSL Certificate Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Certificate not provisioned
- DNS not configured
- Certificate expired
- Certificate authority error

## How to Fix

### Provision Certificate

1. Go to Domain Settings
2. Click HTTPS
3. Verify DNS configuration
