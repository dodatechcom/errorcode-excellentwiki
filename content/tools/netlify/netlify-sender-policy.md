Netlify Sender Policy Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Sender domain not configured
- SPF check failed
- DKIM not enabled
- DMARC policy blocking

## How to Fix

### Configure Email

1. Go to Domain Settings > Email
2. Configure sender policy
