Netlify Role-Based Access Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Role not assigned
- Permission denied
- Role does not exist
- Role change not saved

## How to Fix

### Assign Role

1. Go to Identity > Users
2. Click on user
3. Add role
