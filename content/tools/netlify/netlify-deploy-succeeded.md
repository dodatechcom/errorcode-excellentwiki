Netlify Deploy Succeeded Notification Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Notification not triggered
- Email not received
- Slack notification failed

## How to Fix

### Configure Success Notification

1. Go to Deploy notifications
2. Add deploy succeeded notification
