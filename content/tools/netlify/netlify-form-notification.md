Netlify Form Notification Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Notification not sent
- Email address invalid
- Notification service unavailable

## How to Fix

### Configure Notification

1. Go to Forms > Notifications
2. Add notification
3. Configure email recipient
