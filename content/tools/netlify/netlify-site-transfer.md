Netlify Site Transfer Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Transfer not allowed
- Target team not found
- Transfer already pending
- Permission denied

## How to Fix

### Transfer Site

1. Go to Site Settings > General
2. Click Transfer site
