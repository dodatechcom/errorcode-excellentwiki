Netlify Signup Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Signup not enabled
- Email confirmation required
- Invite only mode
- Rate limit exceeded

## How to Fix

### Enable Signup

1. Go to Identity > Settings
2. Enable Email or external providers
