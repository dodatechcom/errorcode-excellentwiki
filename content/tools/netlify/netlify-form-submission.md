Netlify Form Submission Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Submission not received
- Form name mismatch
- CAPTCHA required
- Form exceeds size limit

## How to Fix

### Check Form

1. Go to Forms tab
2. Verify form is detected
3. Check submissions
