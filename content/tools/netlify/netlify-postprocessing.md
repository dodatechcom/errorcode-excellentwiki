Netlify Postprocessing Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Postprocessing failed
- Output not generated
- Processing timeout

## How to Fix

### Check Postprocessing

1. Go to Site Settings > Build & deploy
2. Check postprocessing settings
