Netlify Certificate Auto-Renew Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Auto-renewal failed
- DNS changed after issuance
- Certificate authority unreachable
- Validation failed

## How to Fix

### Check Status

1. Go to Domain Settings > HTTPS
2. Check renewal status

### Force Renewal

Contact Netlify support if auto-renewal fails.
