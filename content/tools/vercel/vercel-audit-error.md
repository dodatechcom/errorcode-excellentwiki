Vercel Audit Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Audit log not accessible
- Log retention expired
- Permission denied
- Log query too broad

## How to Fix

### Access Logs

1. Go to Team Settings > Audit Log
2. Filter by date and action
