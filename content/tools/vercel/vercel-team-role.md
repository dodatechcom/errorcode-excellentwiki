Vercel Team Role Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Role not assigned correctly
- Insufficient permissions
- Role does not exist
- Role change requires admin

## How to Fix

### Assign Role

1. Go to Team Settings > Members
2. Click on member
3. Change role
