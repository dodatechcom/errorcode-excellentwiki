Vercel SSL Provisioning Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- SSL certificate pending
- Domain not verified
- DNS not propagated
- Certificate authority error

## How to Fix

### Check Status

1. Go to Project Settings > Domains
2. Check SSL status

### Wait

SSL provisioning can take up to 24 hours.
