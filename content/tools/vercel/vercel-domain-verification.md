Vercel Domain Verification Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- DNS record not found
- TXT record missing
- Verification timed out
- Domain already verified by another account

## How to Fix

### Verify Domain

1. Go to Project Settings > Domains
2. Click Verify
3. Add DNS record
