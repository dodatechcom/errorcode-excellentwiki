Vercel Team Invite Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Invite link expired
- Email address invalid
- Team member limit reached
- Invitation already accepted

## How to Fix

### Send Invite

1. Go to Team Settings > Members
2. Click Invite
3. Enter email address
