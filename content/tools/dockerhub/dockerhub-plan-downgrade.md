DockerHub Plan Downgrade Error can prevent your application from working correctly.

## Common Causes

- Downgrade failed
- Feature loss
- Data exceeds plan limits

## How to Fix

### Downgrade Plan

1. Go to Account Settings > Billing
2. Change plan
